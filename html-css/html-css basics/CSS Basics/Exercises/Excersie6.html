Pasta Rosa

Pasta Rosa is a creamy and delicious pasta dish made with tomato sauce and cream. You can read more about pasta here.

Ingredients

  • 500g pasta
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup cooking cream
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Salt and pepper

Preparation

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions.
  2. Heat the tomato sauce in a pan.
  3. Add the cooking cream and mix well.
  4. Add garlic, salt and pepper.
  5. Mix the sauce with the pasta and serve hot.
